Our Inspiration:
Raginee Gaur was a very ambitious girl from the beginning, a multi faceted personality and wanted to achieve a lot of goals. She was fearless, extrovert and always ready to try something challenging.
She was always in a hurry as if she knew that time is running out for her. She was very strong from outside but inside her was a very emotional and divine soul. She was very kind to animals and children, and trusted anyone whether friend or foe.
Born and brought up in a very reputed family, she had all the good qualities and acquired many expertises from the very beginning. She learnt to play hockey on skates (Roller Hockey) during early teenage and played state level tournaments. She was very good in playing chess and dart games and won a few tournaments. She was also a very good pencil drawing artist with ability to make real-life portraits. She had a deep interest in spirituality, astrology and creativeness, but could not save herself from the addiction of partying and smoking.
She had many friends right from her childhood but slowly and gradually all have moved on leaving her in despair. She lived her life on own terms, and left this world as she has come, alone...
She had a pet named "Ninni" (Cat) which she had found on the streets, and kept it for eight years. Perhaps this is the longest association anyone had with her, other than parents and sibling.
In later part of her life she became obsessed with music. She acquired professional training in disk jockeying and started creating her own audio tracks. Some of them are hosted on mix-cloud and You-Tube.
